Making Domestic Violence More Known

Domestic Violence is a topic in the state of Kentucky, and all over the United States that does not get enough attention. In 2018 there was a combined number of 22,546 people that were getting some kind of treatment/recovery method from asstiance places within the state of Kentucky. This topic doesn't need to be avoided, it needs to be brought up more. People need to be aware, and that is how change is sparked. The results shown, help prove the statistics that men are more responisble for domestic violence, but not completely. People of all races, classes, and genders are falling victim.
